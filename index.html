Welcome to "LinkDaBox," a demo app authored by Stan Mohler.

Built on ReactJS, ReduxJS, and NodeJS, this app lets you author graphs of linked textboxes.

  • Double-click anywhere in the large gray region below, to create an empty but editable textbox.
  • Drag textboxes around using the dragbar. Resize via the lower-right corner.
  • Press the link button on one, then on the dragbar of another to link them.
  • Delete textboxes and links, too.
  • Source is on GitHub.
